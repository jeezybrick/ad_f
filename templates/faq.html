{% trans "F.A.Q." %}

Why am I seeing an offer to collect a dollar on this website?

The website you are visiting has decided to reward you for your visit and loyalty. The reward being offered is a $5 gift card at a participating retailer or business.

How many times do I need to visit the website to qualify for a reward?

You need to visit the website 5 times over 5 calendar days and you must click on the widget to collect and save your new dollar. Once you have collected $5, you will be prompted to redeem your reward. Only a limited number of rewards are available, on a first come, first redeemed basis.

What if I miss a day and forget to visit the website?

Sign up for email notifications and we will remind you to visit the website! You can visit any day to qualify for your reward, as long as the campaign is still active and the number of cards are still available. Each campaign runs for a specific time period (generally 7-10 days), OR until all rewards have been redeemed (whichever comes first).

Can I qualify to redeem more than one reward?

One reward is redeemable per person, per campaign. Sign up to be notified for future rewards and we will remind you when more rewards from your favourite brands become available!

How often do you offer rewards? I want to participate again.

At the end of a campaign, you can sign up to receive an email notification for when future rewards become available. The website or brand will then notify you when future rewards become available on the website. A valid email address must be provided to receive reminders. Also, remember to add notifications@adfits.com to your “safe senders” list.

What if I don’t like the brand that you are offering a reward for?

We do our best to bring you the best and most popular brand rewards. If you would like to suggest a brand reward, send us a Tweet @ADfits or email us at info@adfits.com and we will do our best to reward you with more of the brands you love.

How long does it take to process redeemed rewards?

Depending on the retailer, rewards will take a minimum of 72 hours to process. All rewards can only be issued to a valid email address.

Can I receive my redeemed reward in the mail?

At this time, rewards can only be redeemed digitally via a valid email address.

Does my reward have an expiration date?

Depending on the retailer, rewards may have a soft expiration date of one year.

Why is the widget still showing $X dollar value?

The dollar value on the widget will only increase once per day per browser. Please do not erase your cookies or visit the website under “private browsing” settings. This will hamper our ability to track your consecutive visits and reward you for your loyalty. Please read our Terms of Use for more details or send an email to Jennifer Williams at info@adfits.com and we will do our best to further assist you.

How can I sign up for reminder email notifications?

Simply enter your email address in the field and press the submit button. Remember to add notifications@adfits.com to your “safe senders” list.

Why are reminder emails not being sent to my email address?

Please add notifications@adfits.com to your safe senders list. The email will be sent from Jennifer Williams of the ADfits team.
